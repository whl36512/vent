Welcome to Beegrove, the Anonymous Comment Hub

Why Anonymous?
Over the years, the political environment has changed. Sometimes when we want to express our opinion, we hesitate in face of retaliation, bullying, ridicule, etc.. This site offers a platform of true freedom of speech, where nobody can find out who you are, even the administrators of this site.

No identity tracking
This site does not require login, does not track your email address, your ip address or any of your identity information

Thoughtful Comments
This site encourages thoughtful and constructive comments. Any comments with curse words are subject to removal. Repeat abusers will be blocked from using the site, temporarily or permanently.
